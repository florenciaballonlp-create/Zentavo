Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 1, 2026

Zentavo respects your privacy. This policy explains how we handle information when you use the app.

1. Financial data

Financial information (expenses, income, categories, budgets, reports) is stored locally on your device.

2. In-app purchases

Premium purchases are processed by Apple App Store or Google Play. Zentavo uses purchase status to unlock Premium features.

3. Ads in free version

The free version may show ads. Ad providers may use device identifiers according to their policies.

4. Permissions

  • Camera: optional QR scan features.
  • Photos: optional attachment/export features.
  • Notifications: reminders configured by the user.

5. Retention and deletion

Data remains on the device until the user deletes it or uninstalls the app.

6. Children

Zentavo is not directed to children under 13.

7. Contact

Privacy questions: soporte@zentavo.it
